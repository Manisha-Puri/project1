About us

Mary’s Cakes and Pastries is a specialty bakery that makes unique cakes for weddings, birthdays, showers and other celebrations — all baked fresh in historic downtown Northport, Alabama. Mary’s Cakes also offers cookies, pastries, beverages, and a daily $5 lunch special. We also offer a selection of paleo and gluten-free pastries. We’re a take-out venue, but there is a small courtyard with a couple of tables just outside the shop. When the weather’s nice, you are welcome to enjoy your goodies there under a shade umbrella.

We’re right next door to Mary’s Cupboard, a consignment bakeware shop.

The bakery is open 9-6 Tuesday through Friday, and 9-3 on Saturday.
